Why DASS-21?

DASS-21 is a commonly used self-report questionnaire that is designed to measure the severity of symptoms related to stress, anxiety, and depression. It has been scientifically validated and widely used in clinical and research settings.

Using DASS-21 in a daily mood tracking tool can provide several benefits. It allows users to monitor their mood over time and identify changes in their mental health. This can help users detect potential issues early on and seek appropriate support if needed.

Tracking mood using a validated tool like DASS-21 can provide users with a more objective assessment of their mental health. This can be especially helpful for individuals who may struggle to accurately assess their own emotions.

DASS-21 is a reliable and valid tool that can provide users with valuable information about their mental health. By incorporating it into a daily mood tracking tool, users can gain insights into their emotional patterns and potentially identify triggers for stress and anxiety.
